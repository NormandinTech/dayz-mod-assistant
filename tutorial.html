01Getting Started

Luna runs entirely in your browser — no install required. You get 20 free messages to try her out.

1
Open the app
Go to normandintech.github.io/dayz-mod-assistant/app.html. No login required.
2
Type your question
Click the Luna@dzmod:~$ input at the bottom and type your question. Press Enter to send.
3
Use the sidebar
Click any item in the left sidebar — Scripts, Config, Vehicles, Tools, Server — to ask a pre-built question instantly.
4
Follow up naturally
Luna remembers the conversation. Ask "show me the full class" or "how do I test this?" and she stays in context.
💡
Free tier tip: Use your 20 messages on complex questions — Luna gives longer, more complete answers than a Google search.
02Asking Good Questions

Luna gives better answers with more context. Be specific about the file, class, and behavior you want.

// Too vague
How do I add loot?
Why isn't my mod working?
// Specific and complete
How do I add a custom military backpack to types.xml
with nominal 5, min 2, lifetime 14400, military zones only?

My modded class override isn't firing OnInit() —
extending ItemBase in 4_World. What am I missing?
🎯
Be specific
Name the file (types.xml), the class (ItemBase), and the exact behavior you want.
📋
Include your code
Paste your current code so Luna can spot the exact issue rather than guessing.
🗺
Mention your map
Some configs differ by map. Tell Luna which map you're running (Chernarus, Banov, Namalsk...).
⚠
Share error messages
Copy the exact error from your RPT log. Luna can diagnose most DayZ errors instantly.
03Uploading Your Mod Files

Click + Upload files in the toolbar to add your actual mod files. Luna reads the real content and gives advice based on your code.

1
Click "+ Upload files"
Supports .c, .cpp, .xml, .json, .cfg, .sqf, .txt, .hpp and more.
2
Files appear as tags
Uploaded files show as orange tags. Luna references them in every response until removed.
3
Ask about your code
Ask "review my types.xml for errors" or "why isn't my modded class firing?" — Luna reads the actual file.
💾
Files persist across sessions in your browser's local storage.
04Loading PBO Files

Click 📦 Load PBO to inspect any DayZ mod PBO. Text files are extracted, and binarized config.bin files are auto-decompiled.

1
Click "📦 Load PBO"
Select a .pbo file. It's parsed entirely in your browser — nothing is uploaded to any server.
2
Browse the file tree
All text files are listed. Binary files like .p3d models are automatically skipped.
3
Add files to context
Select files and click "Add to Context". Luna can then answer questions about the mod's code.
05Using Pinned Context

The 📌 Context bar injects persistent info into every message. Pin your server details once and Luna uses them all session.

// Good things to pin:
"Banov map, DayZ 1.25, Dr. Jones Trader, 20 player server"
"Custom vehicle mod, extending CarScript, need inventory access"
"Server crashing on startup, Namalsk, mods: CF, VPP, COT"
06Community Contribute

The 🌐 Contribute button shares your uploaded mod files with the community knowledge base, helping Luna improve for all users.

⚠
Only contribute your own files. Don't share files from other mods without permission.

08FAQ
Does Luna remember previous sessions?
Chat history is restored from browser local storage when you return. Luna won't remember conversations from weeks ago.
What DayZ version does Luna support?
Luna is current with DayZ 1.25 Enforce Script, the latest CE format, and current DayZ Tools.
Can Luna write complete mods for me?
Yes — ask for config.cpp, script files, and economy configs separately. Break large mods into pieces.
Is my uploaded code private?
Files are stored in your browser only and sent to the AI for processing. Nothing is stored on a server unless you click Contribute.
My license key isn't working.
Paste the full key exactly as provided by Gumroad. If it still fails, email vegabotgg@outlook.com with your order number.
